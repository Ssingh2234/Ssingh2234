Gurdas Maan: The Legend of Punjabi Music

Biography

Gurdas Maan was born on 4 January 1957 in Giddarbaha, Punjab, India. He gained national attention in 1980 with the song "Dil Da Mamla Hai".

I will continue to serve Punjabi music till my last breath. - Gurdas Maan

Career

Gurdas Maan has recorded over 34 albums and written over 305 songs. His song "Apna Punjab Hove" is considered an anthem.

Music is the language of the soul. - Gurdas Maan
